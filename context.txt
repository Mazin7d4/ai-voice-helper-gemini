Progress Update — March 10, 2026
===================================

Our work is going well — we have good progress.

The main remaining issue is click accuracy. The system got stuck because it
couldn't click on folders correctly in some cases.

Example: When asked to save a Notepad document to the Desktop, the vision
model navigated to the C: drive instead, because "Desktop" wasn't visible
in the Quick Access sidebar at that moment. We got stuck due to click
inaccuracy on the folder tree.

However, most clicks and core functions are much better than before:
- When asked to save to the Downloads folder, it DID correctly click on
  Downloads from the Quick Access sidebar.
- It then couldn't accurately click the Save button — minor improvement needed.

Overall the system is functional and improving. Click precision and task
logic need minor refinement, which we can address later.
